The Current COLA Challenge

TTB receives over 100,000 label approval applications per year. The manual review process creates bottlenecks that slow down the entire alcohol beverage industry.

30–60

Days Average Review Time

Producers wait up to two months before knowing if their label is approved — delaying product launches and market entry.

~40%

Application Rejection Rate

Nearly half of all COLA applications are rejected — most for predictable, avoidable errors that a pre-check could catch instantly.

100+

Regulatory Rules to Check

27 CFR Parts 4, 5, and 7 contain hundreds of specific labeling requirements. Manually checking each one is time-consuming and error-prone.

2×

Resubmission Cycle Cost

Each rejection doubles the wait time. Producers redesign, resubmit, and wait again — sometimes through multiple rejection cycles.
